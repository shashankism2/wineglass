Cube Calculator


The Exponent Calculator helps you calculate the value of a base number raised to a power. Exponents are a way of expressing repeated multiplication. For example, an means multiplying the base a exactly n times.

Exponent Formula

an = a × a × a × ... (n times)

Where:

  • a = Base number
  • n = Exponent (number of times the base is multiplied)
  • an = Result of exponentiation

How It Works

Simply enter the base value and the exponent, then click the calculate button. The calculator instantly computes the result using:
Value = BaseExponent.

Examples

Example 1:

Calculate 23
2 × 2 × 2 = 8
Result: 8

Example 2:

Calculate 52
5 × 5 = 25
Result: 25

Example 3:

Calculate 104
10 × 10 × 10 × 10 = 10000
Result: 10000
