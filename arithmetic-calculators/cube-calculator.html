Cube Calculator

Enter a number:


The Cube Calculator helps you easily find the cube of any number. The cube of a number is the value obtained when the number is multiplied by itself three times. This tool works for whole numbers, decimals, positive values, and negative values, giving quick and accurate results.

The formula for finding the cube of a number is:

Cube = x³ = x × x × x

Examples

  • Input: 2
    Cube: 2³ = 2 × 2 × 2 = 8

  • Input: -4
    Cube: (-4)³ = -4 × -4 × -4 = -64

  • Input: 3.5
    Cube: 3.5³ = 3.5 × 3.5 × 3.5 = 42.875
